The Khojak Project

About the Application

The thought process behind this project is that information is the key. Delivering right information at the right time can boost usage of public transport system. Encouraging people to use public transport by removing the uncertainty of public transport with GPS enabled buses and removing congestion by Points System, Cheapest & Safest Carpooling, Card less journeys using NFC. Security and quality are maintained by KYC verification and review. This shall help government to understand people’s mobility needs, public transport usage & adaptability towards changes. In this report, the process of execution of the above-mentioned ideas and their use-cases is carefully explained including each and every process’ implementation and their respective dependencies.

Modules (sub-features) to be implemented:
  • Custom Bus-Search
  • Journey Planner
  • Smart Carpooling
  • Emmission Score Meter
  • NFC-ify feature
